The Essence of Velvet Noire

Velvet Noire is a fragrance that embodies luxury and mystery. Inspired by the velvet-clad evenings of Parisian high society, this scent is both bold and sophisticated.

The fragrance opens with a sparkling burst of bergamot and pink pepper, leading to a heart of voluptuous jasmine and rose. The dry-down reveals a warm, sensual base of vanilla, patchouli, and amber that lingers on the skin for hours.

How to Wear

Apply to pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears) for optimal diffusion. The high concentration of fragrance oils means 2-3 sprays are sufficient for all-day wear. Perfect for evening events, special occasions, or whenever you want to make a lasting impression.
