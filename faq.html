Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I choose the right fragrance?

Choosing a fragrance is a personal experience. Consider the occasions you'll wear it, your personality, and the scents you're naturally drawn to. Our fragrance quiz can help guide you to scents that match your preferences. You can also order samples to try before committing to a full bottle.

What's the difference between Eau de Parfum and Eau de Toilette?

Eau de Parfum (EDP) has a higher concentration of fragrance oils (15-20%) and typically lasts longer on the skin (6-8 hours). Eau de Toilette (EDT) has a lower concentration (5-15%) and generally lasts 4-6 hours. EDPs are more intense, while EDTs are lighter and often more suitable for daytime wear.

How should I store my fragrance?

To preserve your fragrance's quality, store it in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperature changes. Bathrooms are not ideal due to humidity and temperature fluctuations. Original packaging can help protect the fragrance from light exposure.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the US and 7-14 business days for international orders. We also offer express shipping options (2-3 business days) for domestic orders. Processing time is 1-2 business days before shipment. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened and unused products in their original packaging. Due to the nature of fragrance products, we cannot accept returns of opened items unless they're defective. For defective products, please contact our customer service within 14 days of receipt.

Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Yes! All Velvet Aura products are cruelty-free and never tested on animals. Most of our fragrances are vegan, though some do contain ethically sourced beeswax or honey derivatives. Vegan options are clearly marked on product pages.

Can I get samples before purchasing?

Absolutely! We offer sample sets that let you try multiple scents before committing to a full bottle. Samples are also included with every full-size purchase. Additionally, we have a discovery kit that includes our most popular fragrances in travel-size formats.

How can I make my fragrance last longer?

Apply fragrance to pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears) where body heat helps diffuse the scent. Moisturized skin holds fragrance better, so consider applying unscented lotion first. Avoid rubbing wrists together as this breaks down the fragrance molecules. Layering with matching shower gel or lotion can also extend wear time.

Our customer service team is happy to help with any additional questions you may have.
