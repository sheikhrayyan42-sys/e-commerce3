Our Story

Founded in 2010, Velvet Aura began as a small boutique in Paris with a vision to create timeless fragrances that tell a story. Our master perfumers blend the finest ingredients to craft scents that evoke emotion and memories.

Each bottle is a work of art, designed to be as beautiful as the fragrance inside. We believe in sustainable luxury, sourcing ethically and reducing our environmental impact.
